## North Dakota Weather and Your Roof

Bismarck sees some of the harshest roofing conditions in the country. According to National Weather Service Bismarck storm data, the region averages multiple severe hail events each year, with stones often exceeding one inch. Wind gusts above 60 mph are common during summer thunderstorms.

That weather creates three repeat problems:
- **Hail bruising** on asphalt shingles, which voids granule coverage and shortens roof life
- **Wind uplift** that lifts or tears tabs, especially on older 3-tab shingles
- **Ice dams** at the eaves when attic insulation and ventilation are off
