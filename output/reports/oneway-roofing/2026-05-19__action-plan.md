## Houston Roofing FAQs

**What should I look for in a Houston roofing company?**
Look for an active Texas contractor registration, manufacturer certifications (GAF, Owens Corning, or CertainTeed), proof of general liability insurance, written warranties on both labor and materials, and local references from Houston-area homeowners. Oneway Roofing provides all of these on request.

**Can you recommend the best roofing material for Houston weather?**
For most Houston homes, a Class 4 impact-rated asphalt shingle like GAF Timberline HDZ with WindProven coverage or Owens Corning Duration Storm offers the best balance of price, hail resistance, and insurance discount eligibility. Standing-seam metal performs even better against wind but costs roughly 2–3x more.

**How long does a roof replacement take in Houston?**
A standard single-family asphalt shingle replacement in Houston takes 1–2 days once materials are delivered. Tile or metal roofs take 3–7 days. Weather delays during hurricane season can extend timelines.

**How do I file a hail damage claim with my insurance?**
The Texas Department of Insurance recommends filing as soon as possible after the storm. Take dated photos, save any debris, and call a licensed roofer for a written damage report before the adjuster visit. Oneway Roofing meets with your adjuster on-site at no charge.

**What does a new roof cost in Houston TX?**
Most Houston asphalt shingle replacements fall between $9,000 and $22,000 depending on roof size, pitch, layers being removed, and decking repairs needed. Metal and tile cost more. We provide line-item written estimates so you can compare apples to apples.

**Do you handle commercial roofing in Houston?**
Yes. We install and repair TPO, modified bitumen, and other low-slope systems for retail, office, warehouse, and multi-family buildings across Houston, Katy, Sugar Land, Pearland, and Spring.

**Is my roof old enough to need replacement?**
Most three-tab asphalt shingles in Houston last 15–20 years; architectural shingles last 20–30. UV exposure and storm history shorten that window. If your roof is over 15 years old or has survived multiple hailstorms, a free inspection is worth scheduling.

**What is the FORTIFIED Roof program and is it worth it?**
FORTIFIED is an IBHS standard that adds sealed roof decks, ring-shank nails, and enhanced edge detailing to resist hurricane wind. Many Texas insurers offer premium discounts for FORTIFIED roofs. We are certified to install to this standard.

**Do you offer financing for Houston roof replacements?**
Yes. We offer financing options for qualified homeowners, including 0% promotional terms on approved credit. Ask during your estimate.

**What areas around Houston do you serve?**
Houston TX, Katy TX, Sugar Land TX, Pearland TX, and Spring TX, plus surrounding communities inside the Grand Parkway. Call to confirm coverage in your ZIP code.
